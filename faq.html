Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of bicycles do you sell? ➕

We offer a variety of bicycles including mountain bikes, road bikes and hybrid bikes.

2. Do you offer bicycle repair services? ➕

Yes, we provide a full range of bicycle repair services, from simple tune-ups to major repairs. Visit our shop for more information.

3. Can I order a custom bicycle? ➕

Absolutely! We offer custom-built bicycles tailored to your specifications. Contact us for more details.

4. Do you ship bikes internationally? ➕

We currently offer shipping within the country. International shipping is available on certain products, please check with us directly for more information.

5. What is your return policy? ➕

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items. For bikes, returns are only accepted if the bike is unused and in original condition.
