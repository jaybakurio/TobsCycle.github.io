1. Warranty Coverage

TobsCycle provides the following warranty coverage for our products:

  • Frame: Lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Components: 2 years warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Electronics: 1 year warranty against manufacturing defects

Component-Specific Coverage

  • Frame & Fork: Lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Drivetrain Components: 2 years (chains, cassettes, derailleurs)
  • Brake Systems: 2 years (calipers, levers, hydraulic systems)
  • Wheels & Hubs: 2 years (excluding normal wear on bearings)
  • Electronic Systems: 1 year (batteries, displays, sensors)
  • Paint & Finish: 2 years against peeling and bubbling

2. What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Structural integrity issues
  • Component failures under normal use
  • Paint and finish defects
  • Weld failures
  • Frame alignment issues
  • Material defects in carbon fiber components
  • Bearing failures under normal use

3. What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Accidents, crashes, or impacts
  • Unauthorized modifications or alterations
  • Improper assembly or maintenance
  • Use in competitive events
  • Regular maintenance items (brake pads, chains, tires, etc.)
  • Damage from improper storage or transportation
  • Damage from exposure to extreme weather conditions
  • Damage from improper cleaning methods
  • Damage from use of non-TobsCycle parts or accessories

4. Product Registration

To activate your warranty, please register your product within 30 days of purchase:

  • Visit www.tobscycle.com/register
  • Enter your product serial number
  • Upload your proof of purchase
  • Complete your contact information

Note: Unregistered products will have a reduced warranty period of 1 year.

5. Maintenance Requirements

To maintain warranty coverage, regular maintenance is required:

  • Annual professional service check
  • Regular cleaning and lubrication
  • Proper storage in dry conditions
  • Use of recommended cleaning products
  • Following the maintenance schedule in your user manual

6. Warranty Claim Process

Step 1: Contact Customer Service

Contact our customer service team with your warranty claim:

  • Email: support@tobscycle.com
  • Phone: (123) 456-7890
  • Online Form: www.tobscycle.com/warranty-claim

Step 2: Provide Required Information

  • Proof of purchase (receipt or invoice)
  • Product serial number
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Photos of the defect (if applicable)
  • Maintenance records (if requested)
  • Product registration confirmation

Step 3: Assessment and Resolution

  • Our team will assess your claim within 5 business days
  • If approved, we will provide repair or replacement options
  • Shipping costs for warranty claims are covered by TobsCycle
  • Repair time typically 7-14 business days
  • Replacement parts will be of equal or better quality

Important Notes

  • This warranty is valid only for the original purchaser
  • Warranty is non-transferable
  • Regular maintenance is required to maintain warranty coverage
  • Warranty claims must be made within the coverage period
  • Keep all maintenance records and receipts
  • Do not attempt repairs yourself - contact authorized service centers
  • Warranty does not cover shipping damage - contact carrier directly

7. International Warranty

For international customers:

  • Warranty coverage varies by country
  • Contact local authorized dealers for service
  • Shipping costs may not be covered for international claims
  • Local laws may affect warranty terms
